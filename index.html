Brief Description

Embark on an extraordinary opportunity with this expansive ten-acre gem of land nestled within the serene embrace of Bhagrattie Trace, easily accessible via Cunapo and Southern Main Road, Biche, on the captivating island of Trinidad.

This exceptional property seamlessly marries the allure of untouched nature with a myriad of development possibilities, making it an enticing proposition for those in search of their own paradise in the heart of Biche, Trinidad. Picture your aspirations coming to life on this canvas of boundless potential.

Valuable Trees and Versatile Structures: Discover an old red shed on the premises, a diamond in the rough awaiting your transformative touch.The landscape is adorned with cedar and mahogany trees, combining in value to an impressive $200,000. A variety of other captivating trees also grace the property.

Foundation for Versatility: At the core of the property lies a substantial 40 x 40 concrete foundation, the cornerstone for your vision. Upon this sturdy base, an inviting Liming Area, practical Kitchen Area, and a charming 15 x 15 Camping Hut offer comfort and adaptability, perfect for a range of outdoor activities.

Enchanting Pond, Ideal for Duck Farming: A delightful natural pond graces the land, its dimensions stretching 20 feet in width and descending to a depth of 15 feet. This water haven is ideal for realizing your duck farming ambitions, inviting you to create a thriving aquatic farm.

Thoughtful Amenities: Enhancing the property, a meticulously designed toilet facility comes complete with its own cesspit, ensuring convenience in the midst of nature’s embrace.

This land not only holds promise for potential development but also a purposeful base for your overnight expeditions. Embrace the fusion of nature’s elegance and human ingenuity as you explore the prospects that this remarkable property unfolds.

Agriculture: The land has fertile soil that is suitable for agriculture. Tests were done with peppers and pumpkins, yielding good results.

Prime Location and Accessibility: Strategically positioned, a mere ten-minute drive from the bustling Biche Police Station and the primary business district, this property harmoniously merges convenience with a tranquil escape from urban life, capturing the best of both worlds.

Security: The land is located at the end of the trace and benefits from vigilant neighboring residents, including farmers and police patrols.

Price and Ownership: Available at $1.5 million TT or $200,000.00 US, this property offers a negotiable investment opportunity. It’s essential to underline that the land is freehold, backed by meticulous documentation, including the “Certificate of Title,” readily available to facilitate your ownership journey.
